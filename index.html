About #WomenWhoCanCode

-By Women, For Women & To Women Community

#WomenWhoCanCode-organized By: DERBI Foundation

DERBI Foundation is a not for profit Technology Business Incubator,recognised and supported by Department of Science & technology, Government of India.

We, @ DERBI are celebrating the International Women's Day on March 8th 2016 with a Coding Hackathon, Powered By: Dexter Talent Labs.

A wonderful opportunity for all the women hackers to showcase their talent and get recognized.

Meet like minded women and develop awesome application for your community.

Win Awesome Goodies and Have a satisfied day.

We would like to invite all women who can code , code for a cause , code for a solution to be a part of this event.

Awesome prizes for top three hack. Keep Calm and Hack On!!
