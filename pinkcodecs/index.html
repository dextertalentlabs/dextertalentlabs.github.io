Frequently Asked Questions .. ??

Q. Who all can participate ?
Answer : One who can code and develop mobile application on Android and iOS Platforms.

Q. Is it Individual or Team event ?
Answer : You can participate as individual or form a team of Maximum 3 members.

Q. If I don't have idea, Can I still participate ?
Answer : Yes please. We will give you hack idea.

Q. What should I bring ?
Answer : Get your laptop, cellphone, chargers and Dongle or Data Card (for internet connection). In addition, bringing a water bottle, pillow, and any other accessories you would bring for a sleepover would be a good idea.

Q. What software should I use ?
Answer : Well, you may use any programming language you are a master of and which is useful for implementation of your hack idea.

Q. Will I get a certificate ?
Answer : Yes, You will receive a certificate and indeed, a whole new experience.
